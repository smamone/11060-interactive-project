About this Website

This website amalgamates the work completed by students in 11060 Designing for Data Informed User Experience at the University of Canberra, Faculty of Arts and Design 2020.

Spotlight on Suburbia

Stefanie Mamone

Discover the origins and more of the suburbs of Canberra.

Trove Search - Books, Maps and Newspapers

Kim Hughes

This website allows a user to search the Trove database for a book, map or newspaper, display summary details of results matching the query and provide a link to the full details of the result in Trove.

Detective De La Flore

Sarah Dux

This website uses data collected from the Trefle and Atlas of Living Australia APIs, helping users discover flora recorded in their area.

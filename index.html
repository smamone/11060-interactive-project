AmalGLAMation

About this Website

A collaborative space to dive and immerse in to divergent websites. It is an area to get away from boredom. Submerge in unexplored areas that you may not have found entertaining before. Get additional information about topics you want to discover more about and enjoy the novel visuals of each of the collaborations.

AMALGAMATION is a tribute to GLAM: a collection of data held by cultural institutions in a digitized way. It is a collaboration of students from the University of Canberra finding their way through coding. Unifying a project that brings together knowledge from books, manuscripts, photographs, objects, and much more.... Come and join us!
